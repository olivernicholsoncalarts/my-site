Oliver Muco Nicholson: Portfolio

Oliver is a multi-instrumentalist, specialising as a vocalist. He also plays the piano, viola and the Koto (Japanese harp). He has performed his original compositions in the United Kingdom, Burundi, Rwanda, Portugal, and the United States, including opening for Sudanese-Canadian artist Emmanuel Jal and Rwandan singer Meddy Saleh as well as collaborations with musicians from East-Africa including Deejay Pius and Amalon.

His solo work, which he releases under the name 'Muco’ (pronounced Moo-Cho) has been streamed over 1.5 million times worldwide and has been described as “charming”, “compelling”, featuring a “kaleidoscope of contrasts” by Come Here Floyd. DNÜ says Muco finds “a balance between being haunting and spiritual”, with “dreamy production making room for stunning vocals”.
